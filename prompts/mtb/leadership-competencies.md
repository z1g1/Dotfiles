# M&T Bank Leadership Competencies

These eight competencies are the "how" behind performance and what each leader should possess. Learn more about each of them and incorporate them into your performance objectives, development items, and daily interactions.

A Leader at M&T...

## Creates Vision
Sets priorities and aligns people to them
### Team Member 
Sets and aligns their own priorities to the bank’s strategy and purpose
### Manager / Team Leader
Connects their team’s actions and priorities to the bank’s strategy and purpose
### Multi-Discipliary Leader
Aligns disparate teams around a common vision and core set of priorities
### Whole/Business Leader 
Creates an integrated strategy and vision for the whole business
### Enterprise Leader
Establishes bank-wide vision and priorities that will shape the future

## Instipres Followers
Energizes and mobilizes others to take action
### Team Member 
Earns trust and respect through expertise and authentic connection
### Manager / Team Leader
Positively motivates and energizes team; inspires people to take action
### Multi-Discipliary Leader
Motivates and inspires people from different areas of expertise
### Whole/Business Leader 
Demonstrates a distinct leadership brand amongst senior leaders
### Enterprise Leader
Mobilizes the bank and the industry; is “one to watch and listen to”

## Builds Alliances
Creates opportunity through partnership and collaboration
### Team Member 
Builds mutual support and alignment with colleagues
### Manager / Team Leader
Works collaboratively with other teams
### Multi-Discipliary Leader
Intentionally collaborates across traditional silos to unlock value
### Whole/Business Leader 
Builds strategic partnerships inside and outside the bank
### Enterprise Leader
Creates enduring value for the bank through M&A and alliances

## Influences
Gains buy-in without the use of position
### Team Member 
Understands the needs of different types of stakeholders
### Manager / Team Leader
Adapts approaches to build support from key stakeholders
### Multi-Discipliary Leader
Creates buy-in from people with disparate backgrounds/interests
### Whole/Business Leader 
Leverages relationships with powerful people to advance our strategy
### Enterprise Leader
Influences the industry, market, regulators, brand and community

## Delivers Results
Drives agility and accountability to elevate performance
### Team Member 
Embraces agile ways of working and personal accountability for results
### Manager / Team Leader
Fosters agile ways of working among team to increase performance
### Multi-Discipliary Leader
Stretches performance of people with a disparate range of accountabilities
### Whole/Business Leader 
### Enterprise Leader
Builds results-oriented culture of accountability for the whole business
### Enterprise Leader
Holds top leaders accountable for driving enterprise results

## Develops Talent
Develops the future workforce for competitive advantage
### Team Member 
Owns and drives own professional development
### Manager / Team Leader
Coaches and develops people and teams to build critical capabilities
### Multi-Discipliary Leader
Creates development opportunities to build wide-ranging skill sets
### Whole/Business Leader 
Sponsors organizational talent to strengthen the pipeline of senior leaders
### Enterprise Leader
Leads the bank to build enduring capabilities for competitive advantage

## Innovates
Embraces new and different ideas and fosters a culture of change
### Team Member 
Seeks out ideas and best practices; embraces change
### Manager / Team Leader
Encourages new ideas and helps the team adapt to change
### Multi-Discipliary Leader
Creates conditions for change and innovation across disparate/fluid teams
### Whole/Business Leader 
Drives the transformation agenda for the whole business
### Enterprise Leader
Defines a transformation agenda that expands the market/business model

## Grows Value 
Understands our customers and the business of banking 
### Team Member 
Analyzes data and creates insights that deliver business value
### Manager / Team Leader
Makes informed decisions to allocate resources effectively
### Multi-Discipliary Leader
Leverages all P&L and risk drivers to maximize commercial return
### Whole/Business Leader 
Secures a return on any capital investment for the whole business
### Enterprise Leader
Makes sound decisions on what will grow the value of the enterprise i.e., for shareholders
